Drunken Walk

Life is a random walk highly sensitive to initial condition, in which my realised path has largely been sub-optimal. After studying the wrong subjects at the wrong schools and working for the wrong companies in the wrong industry, I decided to deprecate life 1.0 and branch into life 2.0.

Technology and mathematics are at the centre of life 2.0. Time is spent thinking about ideas and working on things rather than cultivating corporate relationships. If I ever again have to work using my mouth rather than my brain, I might as well be a prostitute.

My intellectual interests center around probability, random processes, econometrics, causal inference, probabilistic graphical models, and statistical learning.

Bayesian Bread and Butter

I think risky inductive reasoning is much more appropriate than objective deductive logic in learning about and navigating in a chaotic environment. I believe objective truth is a rarity and the only attainable truth is normative, established through intuition, abstraction, and observations.

In this noisy world, much of our perceived knowledge is bound to be spurious; the best we can do is to embrace the inherient randomness and nonlinearity and to remain parsimonious.

True causal understanding is elusive without the luxury of interventions. It is the ability to reason with hypotheses against empirical data and to quantify the state of knowledge in terms of ignorance that makes Bayesian so ubiquitous and pervasive; and for how long I can refrain from the temptation of becoming an empirical Bayes is a computational question.

Technology is Democracy

Fairness and democracy are theoretical ideals in this structurally rigged world. Others have more money, more power, more connections. We are underdogs. We work much harder for much less. They do business on relationship. My data schemas are already relational. They call their connections for favours. My codes call APIs for data. They like networking and handshaking with who's who. My programs are constantly networking and handshaking with data servers.

All I have are a pair of working hands, a functioning brain, and a von Neumann machine. Imagination is innate. Knowledge is unbiased. Technology is empowerment. Data simulate experience. These are my sole weapons to levelling the playing field.
